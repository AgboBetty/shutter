About Franklin

Franklin Ogalanya is a photographer/Filmmaker Based in Enugu Nigeria. With over 7 years of media experience, he has worked on many media projects, From short films to web contents. He Produced and directed his first feature film “Eighteen {18}” in 2017. worked on “Venj” Nigerias first remote film with Rose Okeke, Directed “Who’s coming for you” and “Locked In” . He is currently the Lead visual strategist for Utopian Healthcare and is the Editor and AD for “God’s Wife”, a film by Dika Ofoma. He tells compelling and authentic stories,, writes screenplays and manages content.

He has a deep understanding of photo and video editing softwares for the best practices and delivers all project to completion.

Services

I create cohesive visual narratives and handle all aspects of video production, delivering high-quality content for your brand.

Brand Visual Strategist

I develop comprehensive visual media strategies aimed at increasing traffic to media and web pages, effectively conveying companies' narratives in a relatable manner. My responsibilities includes creating mood boards, generating content concepts, overseeing shooting and production, repurposing videos, collaborating closely with social media teams to analyze outcomes, coordinating and compensating models, and video editing, among other duties.

Studio Manager

I design and set up studio spaces, ensuring optimal functionality and aesthetic appeal. This involves purchasing state-of-the-art equipment tailored to various photographic needs and conducting thorough interviews to select potential assistants who align with the studio's vision. I meticulously plan and conduct shoots, collaborating with clients to achieve their desired outcomes. Creating mood boards is an essential part of my process.

Filmmaker

I offer comprehensive filmmaking services, including scriptwriting, pre-production planning, directing, cinematography, and post-production editing. My expertise spans creating compelling narratives for commercials, documentaries, short films, and feature films. We manage every aspect of the filmmaking process to deliver high-quality visual storytelling that captivates and engages your audience,ensuring your vision is brought to life with creativity.
